Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-05

1. Data we don't collect

Every tool on Utilify (JSON formatter, base64 converter, JWT decoder, hash generator, image converter, PDF tools, etc.) processes your input entirely in the browser. We do not upload, store, or read the content you paste, type, or upload.

2. Cookies & advertising

Utilify is monetized through display advertising delivered by Ezoic, which mediates Google AdSense and other partners. These services may set cookies to personalize ads and measure performance. You can manage consent through the Ezoic CMP banner shown on first visit, and revoke it at any time via the Ezoic privacy controls.

3. Logs

Our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) keeps short-lived access logs containing IP addresses for abuse prevention. We do not access or correlate these logs with any user identity.

4. PWA / offline

Installing Utilify as a Progressive Web App caches static assets for offline use. No personal data is stored.

5. Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You can request information about, or deletion of, any data Ezoic processes about you via their privacy portal. Because Utilify itself does not store user data, requests directed to us will be forwarded to the relevant ad partner.

6. Changes

Updates to this policy will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
