How to use

  1. Enter the original (full) price.
  2. Enter the first discount percent.
  3. Optional: enter a second discount that applies after the first (typical "extra X% at checkout").
  4. Click Calculate. Final price + savings + effective combined discount appear.

Stacked discounts don't add. A 30% then 20% discount is not 50% — it's 1 − (0.7 × 0.8) = 44%. The effective discount field shows the combined rate.
