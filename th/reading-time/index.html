How to use

  1. Paste the text you want to estimate. The calculator counts words automatically (whitespace-split, ignoring extra spacing).
  2. Adjust the words-per-minute (WPM) speed. Common targets:
    • 150 WPM — comfortable spoken / podcast pace
    • 250 WPM — average silent reading (default)
    • 400 WPM — skimming / fast reading
  3. Click Estimate. Both reading time and a separate spoken-time estimate (at 150 WPM) are shown.

Most blog platforms use 200–250 WPM. For audio scripts (podcasts, voiceovers, talks), use 130–160 WPM — slow enough to be clearly understood out loud.
