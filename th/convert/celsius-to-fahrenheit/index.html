Frequently Asked Questions

How many fahrenheit are in 1 celsius?

1 celsius equals 33.8 fahrenheit. °F = (°C × 9/5) + 32

What is the formula to convert celsius to fahrenheit?

°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32. Inverse: °C = (°F − 32) × 5/9.

How many fahrenheit are in 10 celsius?

10 celsius equals 50 fahrenheit.

Is this conversion exact?

Yes, the converter uses the standard definition factor and rounds the displayed result to 2 decimal places.
