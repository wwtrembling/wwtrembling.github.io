How to use

  1. Enter the pre-tip bill amount.
  2. Pick a quick tip percentage or type a custom one.
  3. Enter how many people are splitting the bill.
  4. Click Calculate — tip, total, and per-person amount appear.

US convention is 18–20% for table service, 15% for buffets/delivery, 10% or rounding-up for fast counter service. Many countries either include service in the bill or don't tip at all — check local custom.
